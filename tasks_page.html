Task 8: Big Data

Produce two charts using the UK price data provided in class.

These charts use the long run price data from super markets to compare the price of a healthy food and unhealthy food over time. The top chart shows the changes in broccoli and a packet of biscuits over time in London. The data is filtered by item then the price is aggregated as the median price for that item. The data is further filtered just for London for chart 1.

This data is from the AutoCPI data and the code can be found on Google Colab.
